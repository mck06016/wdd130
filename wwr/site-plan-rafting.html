Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site will be to advertise White Water Rafting on the Thompson River in British Columbia Canada. This site will share information and provide oportunities to participate in Water Rafting on the Thompson River. The rafting excursions are available in 3 options: Day Trips, Over Night Camping, Week Long Rafting.

Audience

Rafting should be accessable to all ages, however for saftey the intended audience is 18-65, with the addition of 12-18 with parental approval and supervision. These participants will be seeking to participate in white water rafting events and excursions. The expectation is that those seeking information will be seeking from multiple sources and may be looking over a period of time to locate the best experience for their needs. It is also expected users will access the site via mobile or computer browser so formating should be completed for both.
